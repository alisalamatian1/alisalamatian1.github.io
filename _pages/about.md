I am Ali Salamatian, recently graduated from Computer Science Honours at the University of British Columbia. I'm excited to join UT Austin for PhD in the fall.


Previously, I worked on these projects: efficient video patch selection (supervised by [Prof. Leonid Sigal](https://www.cs.ubc.ca/~lsigal/) and [Prof. Evan Shelhamer](https://imaginarynumber.net/)), enhancing vision-language-action models with MCTS (supervised by [Prof. Cyrus Neary](https://www.cyrusneary.com/)), and ChartGaze, aligning LVLMs' attention with human gaze (supervised by [Prof. Giuseppe Carenini](https://www.cs.ubc.ca/~carenini) and [Prof. Leonid Sigal](https://www.cs.ubc.ca/~lsigal/)). Please check the Publications page for more information.

I really enjoy research and collaborating with others so feel free to reach out if you have any interesting ideas!
